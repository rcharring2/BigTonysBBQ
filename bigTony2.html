About Us

We've been described as a "Burlington, North Carolina staple". Big Tony's Rib and 'Q' shack was opened in 1956 by the original Big Tony. Now the 'Q' Shack is run by his son, Big Tony Jr. We feel that it is our dedication to "old school" 'Q' that has kept us around and thriving for so long but don't take our word for it, stop in yourself to try our 'Q'!
